About

Hello! Thanks for being curious about who I am and what I'm up to. I created this blog to share my personal and professional thoughts & projects, mainly centered around coding and continuous improvement.

I am a developer currently working in data science but my interests and fascinations are varied. I enjoy tinkering with Linux, studying computer science and sharpening my development skills. Outside of technology I enjoy working on my air-cooled Volkswagen Beetle, film photography, playing the banjo and weightlifting.

The CV part:

Eric Ehmke is a data scientist & developer at a multinational aerospace company. He transitioned to the field after a career in manufacturing operations and quality focusing on data analytics. Eric graduated from Central Connecticut State University with a degree in Industrial Technology in 2014 and has a proven track record of creating value through and data-driven process improvements.

Before transitioning to data science, Eric led a team of 25+ operating an advanced metallic and ceramic coatings cell for military & commercial aircraft engine components. Under his management production runs increased by 18% and 27%. He leveraged his development skills to build Python based digital factory tools, aid production tracking, total preventative maintenance scheduling and machine qualification.

Eric has also performed roles in quality engineering mitigating quality risks, analyzing production data and auditing supplier processes. Eric designed and managed contractor teams valued at $280,000 y/r, traveled throughout the United States coaching the PPAP process and interacting with commercial and military customers & suppliers.
